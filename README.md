## Overview

## Key Findings
- **Current RPS**: $18.53 (net of refunds)
- **Total Sessions Analyzed**: 100,000
- **Total Revenue**: $1,938,509.75 (gross)
- **Refund Rate**: 4.40% ($85,338 in refunds)

## Strategic Recommendations
1. **Channel Budget Reallocation** - Reallocate 40% budget from low to high-performing channels
2. **Customer Retention Program** - Focus on repeat customers who generate 29% more revenue per session
3. **Conversion Rate Optimization** - Improve current 32.31% conversion rate by 0.5-1%
4. **Product Bundling Strategy** - Leverage best-seller for cross-sell opportunities
5. **Refund Reduction Program** - Reduce 4.40% refund rate by 30%

## Financial Projections
- **Expected RPS Improvement**: +$2.20 (from $18.53 to $20.73)
- **Percentage Improvement**: 11.88%
- **Annual Revenue Impact**: +$11.4M
- **ROI on Channel Reallocation**: ~600%
