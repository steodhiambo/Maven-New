# Maven Fuzzy Factory - Revenue Optimization Strategy
## Executive Summary

---

## Business Problem Diagnosis

Maven Fuzzy Factory has strong traffic but slow growth, with a current RPS (Revenue per Session) of **$18.53**. The analysis reveals several key areas holding back revenue growth:

- **High refund rate**: 4.40% of revenue is lost to refunds ($85,338)
- **Channel inefficiency**: Significant variation in RPS across traffic channels
- **Customer type disparity**: Repeat customers generate 29% more revenue per session than new customers
- **Product mix optimization opportunities**: The Original Mr. Fuzzy drives 77% of total revenue

---
